Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour: 2 1/4 cups
  • Baking soda: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Unsalted butter, softened: 1 cup (2 sticks)
  • Granulated sugar: 3/4 cup
  • Packed brown sugar: 3/4 cup
  • Vanilla extract: 1 teaspoon
  • Eggs: 2 large
  • Chocolate chips: 2 cups

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour and baking soda. Set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract until light and fluffy.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  6. Gently fold in the chocolate chips until evenly distributed in the cookie dough.
  7. Using a cookie scoop or a tablespoon, drop rounded balls of dough onto ungreased baking sheets, leaving space between each cookie.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are golden brown. The centers may still be soft.
  9. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring them to wire racks to cool completely.
  10. Once cooled, serve and enjoy your homemade chocolate chip cookies with a glass of milk!
